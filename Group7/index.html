Distribution of Students‚Äô Responses on the Positive and Negative Effects of Online Gaming

Summary of Results

The results of our survey and interviews show that stress relief is the top positive effect of online gaming among students. Many respondents explained that gaming helps them relax after school and take a break from academic pressure. Aside from this, students also identified teamwork, problem-solving, and creativity as strong benefits, especially when playing games that require cooperation, strategy, or building. However, the study also revealed challenges, with addiction and poor time management being the main concerns. These findings suggest that while gaming supports valuable skills and well-being, it must be practiced in moderation to avoid negative effects.
