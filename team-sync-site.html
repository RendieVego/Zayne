2) Summary of Stream

The live stream focused on playing a Roblox game while working as a group and interacting with viewers in real time. Each member had a specific role, such as hosting, playing, and monitoring the chat to ensure smooth communication during the stream. The main goal was to entertain viewers while demonstrating teamwork, coordination, and audience engagement. Viewers were acknowledged through comments and reactions, making the experience interactive and enjoyable. Overall, the stream showcased collaborative gameplay and active audience participation.

3) Things Well Done During the Stream

  • Team coordination: Each member understood their role, keeping the stream organized and smooth.
  • Audience interaction: Viewers were acknowledged through comments and reactions, making them feel involved.
  • Technical setup: Video and audio were mostly clear with no major interruptions.

4) Areas of Opportunities

  • Audio balance: Some voices were louder/softer. Adjust mic levels and do a longer sound check.
  • Stream promotion: Announce earlier and encourage sharing to reach more viewers.
  • Clear intro & closing: Use a more structured opening and ending for a more professional feel.

5) Level of Engagement

The engagement was moderate to high. The stream reached 607 views, gained 30 comments, and received 23 reactions (mostly hearts and likes), showing positive feedback. Although there were only 2 shares, the viewers who stayed were responsive. Overall, audience engagement helped the stream feel lively and motivated the group to interact confidently.

6) Viewership Analysis

The stream had high viewership with 607 views. One reason is that the stream was posted using a personal Facebook account, making it easily visible to friends and connections. Another reason is the engaging gameplay, which encouraged viewers to watch and react. However, the low number of shares suggests promotion can still be improved. With better promotion and earlier announcements, the stream could reach an even wider audience in the future.
