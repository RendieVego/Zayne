About Team Sync

We’re Grade 10 students streaming games to highlight teamwork, creativity, and positive online behavior. This website documents our stream outline and promotional plan for our Life Labs Quarter 2 Performance-Based Assessment.

  • Main Platform: Twitch
  • Content: Friendly gaming + short strategy talks + Q&A
  • Goal: Organized streams and consistent growth in viewers

Stream Outline & Schedule

  1. 4:00–4:05 Intro, shoutouts, stream rules
  2. 4:05–4:20 Gameplay + teamwork highlights
  3. 4:20–4:27 Live Q&A / chat interaction
  4. 4:27–4:30 Wrap-up, next stream teaser

Technical Checklist

  • Mic test & audio levels
  • Stable internet (upload > 5 Mbps)
  • Scenes ready (BRB / Live / End)
  • Safety: No personal info on cam
