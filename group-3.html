SECTION 1
Summary of Stream

This paper presents an honest analysis of our livestream titled Stream Squad PH, which was streamed on Twitch for 1 hour and 44 minutes and focused on gaming content. Based on the stream data, the livestream reached 10 unique viewers, with a peak of 6 viewers and an average of 1.3 viewers. There were 2 unique chatters, and no new followers were gained.

Although the stream had some viewers, overall engagement remained low. The atmosphere was not consistently friendly due to inappropriate language used by one group member. This analysis discusses what occurred, what went well, what needs improvement, the engagement level, and possible reasons behind limited viewership.

SECTION 2
Things Well Done During the Stream

Despite the challenges, the group continued the livestream until the end. Even when there were little to no viewers, the members did not stop the stream and still completed the task, showing responsibility and commitment.

Another positive aspect was the basic coordination among some group members. The group was able to manage the gameplay and keep the stream running smoothly, showing cooperation even with low motivation.

SECTION 3
Areas of Opportunities

One major area for improvement was the absence of consistent viewers. With few people watching, there was little audience interaction, making the stream less engaging. Proper promotion and clear scheduling before going live can improve this.

Starting later than scheduled may have affected engagement, since some viewers might have left or missed the opening part. Better preparation and time management can improve credibility and viewer satisfaction.

Another serious concern was the frequent use of inappropriate words during the stream. This created an uncomfortable and unprofessional atmosphere and could discourage viewers from staying or returning.

The overall mood lacked friendliness and positivity. Setting clear rules for behavior and communication before streaming can improve future streams.

SECTION 4
Level of Engagement

Engagement was low. Although the stream reached 10 unique viewers and had 2 unique chatters, interaction remained minimal. The average viewership of 1.3 viewers suggests most viewers did not stay long.

Limited chat activity and an unwelcoming atmosphere reduced meaningful engagement. Inappropriate language also made the stream uncomfortable, which affected overall professionalism.

SECTION 5
Viewership Analysis

The stream had low viewership based on the Twitch summary. While 10 unique viewers visited, average viewership stayed at 1.3 with a peak of 6. This suggests viewers may have joined briefly but did not stay long.

Possible reasons include limited promotion, timing, and the uncomfortable atmosphere caused by inappropriate language. The lack of new followers suggests the stream did not strongly encourage viewers to continue supporting the channel.

Improving content quality, maintaining respectful behavior, and promoting the stream can help increase viewership and engagement in future livestreams.
